Research Areas

What I Work On

LLM-Guided 3D Inference

Using Large Language Models to guide material inference and reasoning over 3D point clouds and CAD models, bridging language understanding with geometric data.

LLMs 3D Point Clouds Multimodal

Boundary Representation

Developing better formats and datasets for B-Rep modeling, including topology-aware meshing and half-edge data structures for CAD applications.

B-Rep STEP Files CAD

Geometric Representation Learning

Learning robust representations of 3D geometric data through multimodal models, combining visual features with structural and material properties.

Deep Learning Representation Learning 3D Data
